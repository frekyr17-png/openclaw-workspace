## 🚫 Filtering Criteria (STRICT - DO NOT COMPROMISE)

### EXCLUDE If:
- [ ] Job title contains: "IT", "CTO", "CIO", "Head of Technology", "Digital", "Software"
- [ ] Company is: Software company, digital agency, tech startup, SaaS business
- [ ] Profile says "we're hiring developers/engineers" (indicates in-house tech)
- [ ] Company has "Technical Co-founder" listed
- [ ] Recent posts about their "tech stack" or "engineering team"

### INCLUDE If:
- [ ] Traditional industry: Manufacturing, Healthcare, Real Estate, Agriculture, D2C Physical Goods
- [ ] Founder/owner without technical background visible
- [ ] Signs of growth but operational struggles
- [ ] Company size: 10-200 employees (sweet spot)
- [ ] No recent tech-heavy hires or "digital transformation" announcements

| Market | Local Business Hours | Best Outreach Times (IST) |
|--------|---------------------|---------------------------|

| Germany | 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM CET | 1:30 PM - 9:30 PM IST |
| France | 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM CET | 1:30 PM - 9:30 PM IST |

| USA West | 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM PST | 10:30 PM - 6:30 AM IST |

- Send connection requests: 2:00-4:00 PM IST (catches Europe mid-day)
- US outreach: Schedule for late night IST (7:30-9:30 PM) or early morning (6:00-8:00 AM)
- Batch by region: Mondays = UK, Tuesdays = Germany, etc.

## 🔍 Quick Research Checklist (Per Prospect)

**2-Minute Profile Audit:**

1. **Company Website Check** (60 seconds):
   - [ ] Website exists? (No website = 🔥 HOT LEAD)
   - [ ] Mobile friendly? (Check on phone)
   - [ ] Load speed? (Slow = opportunity)
   - [ ] Clear CTAs? (Confusing = opportunity)

2. **LinkedIn Profile Deep Dive** (60 seconds):
   - [ ] Recent posts show growth/challenges?
   - [ ] Company size growing? (10-200 employees = ideal)
   - [ ] No tech team visible? (Good sign)
   - [ ] Pain point signals in recent activity?

3. **Gap Analysis** (30 seconds):
   - [ ] Rate 1-10 opportunity score
   - [ ] Note specific pain point mentioned
   - [ ] Draft personalized hook for connection

**Total time per prospect: 2.5 minutes**
